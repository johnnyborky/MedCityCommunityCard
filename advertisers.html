Frequently Asked Questions

Common advertiser questions

What neighborhoods are included?

The card targets Rochester carrier routes aligned to households above $130K, using a premium route footprint and monthly rotation strategy.

Can my competitor appear on the same issue?

No. Category exclusivity is included, so direct competitors are not placed together on the same month’s card.

Can I buy more than one ad space?

Yes. Businesses can reserve multiple spaces to increase their share of visibility on the card.

Do I need finished artwork?

No. You can provide your logo, offer details, and brand assets, and the content can be formatted for the placement.

What kind of businesses are a strong fit?

Businesses that benefit from recurring local reminders tend to be a strong fit, including restaurants, home services, personal care, boutique fitness, and specialty retail.

Can I run coupons or simple brand promotion?

Yes. Advertisers can choose coupon-driven creative or standard promotional messaging depending on their goals.
